FAQ

How do I know that the food is healthy?
All menu items must list nutrition information. As you are making your selections you can group them into meals so that you can see the total calories, fat, etc per meal.
How do I pay for my food?
Healthy Choices integrates with Square allowing you to either pay with Square Wallet or swipe your card on a Square Reader when you pick up your food
